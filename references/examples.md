# Examples

Open this when you need to imitate the final Suno output shape or adapt a compact example.

## Mandarin Ballad

```markdown
1. Lyrics

[Intro: Soft piano]

[Verse 1]
雨停在便利店門口
你的傘還靠在我右手
訊息停在昨天午後
我假裝只是風太久

[Pre-Chorus]
街燈一盞一盞亮起
把沉默照得太清晰

[Chorus]
我還在等一個回頭
等你說那天只是說錯
可城市把答案都收走
只剩我和雨聲拉扯著

[Bridge: Strings]
如果愛能晚一點走
我會先學會放手

[Final Chorus]
我不再等一個回頭
把你的名字還給風

[Outro: Piano fade]
雨停了，我也該走

2. Style of Music
Mandarin pop ballad, melancholic, 74 BPM, soft piano, warm strings, female vocal, intimate emotional production

3. Exclude Styles
EDM, hard rock, metal, rap, live crowd noise

4. Title
雨停之前
```

## English Rap Hook

```markdown
1. Lyrics

[Intro: Beat drop]
Yeah
Count it in

[Rap Verse 1]
I built my name from a locked door knock
No shortcut key, just a loud tick-tock
Hands got dust but the plan stayed clean
Turned one small spark into gasoline

[Hook]
We don't fold, we reload
Step by step on a rough road
If the lights go low, let them all know
We make fire from a cold stone

[Rap Verse 2]
Pressure came with a sharp-tooth grin
I smiled right back and invited it in
Every lost round was a note in red
Now the whole room moves when I nod my head

[Final Hook]
We don't fold, we reload
Step by step on a rough road

[Outro]
Still here
Still loud

2. Style of Music
Boom bap hip-hop, 92 BPM, hard drums, deep bass, confident rap vocal, minimal dark piano loop, punchy hook

3. Exclude Styles
trap hi-hats, pop punk, singing chorus, orchestral, EDM drop

4. Title
Reload
```

## Anti-Pattern And Correction

Weak prompt:

```markdown
Lyrics: Make a song about dreams and light and flying free in the night.
Style of Music: pop rock EDM sad happy
Exclude Styles: no guitars but rock
Title: Song
```

Corrected:

```markdown
1. Lyrics

[Verse 1]
The train leaves at 5:03
Coffee cold beside my knee
I wrote my name on a one-way fare
Like staying here was a dare

[Chorus]
I'm not flying, I'm leaving
One small bag and a reason
If the platform starts to shake
Let it know I chose to break

2. Style of Music
Indie pop rock, 104 BPM, clean electric guitar, tight live drums, warm male vocal, bittersweet but hopeful

3. Exclude Styles
EDM, metal, synthwave, distorted screaming, live crowd

4. Title
5:03
```
